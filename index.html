What is M.A.G.E?

M.A.G.E. (formerly known as MetaCon) is an anime and gaming experience happening in Minneapolis, MN. M.A.G.E. will be an all new experience, blending together Anime, Gaming, SciFi, TV, and adding a little bit of Renaissance and Steampunk inspiration. Come join us to see your favorite Voice Actors from Anime to SciFi and everything in between. Enjoy our Late night dances, our Main Stage Events and extensive gaming departments covering everything from your favorites on console and pc to full one shot DnD campaigns and more!

Location

The Minneapolis Anime and Gaming Event is excited to come to the beautiful Hyatt Regency in downtown Minneapolis. With over 644 guestrooms and 130,000+ square feet of space for the convention, it is the perfect place to hold an epic convention. Here are some highlights: Convention events last until 2am or later! Enjoy events all night long by staying with us! Hotel rooms are only $132/night, making staying at the convention hotel convenient and cheap (rooms downtown are normally $250 a night!). Having a room means you can be part of the action early, and stay up late! Our ConSweet, which includes free ramen, rice, and soda. Attendees have access to free ramen and soda as part of your admission. Our main events room is huge, perfect for an epic rave dance and tons of epic events. All hotel guests even get free wifi!

Parking

We have secured discounted parking rates for all MAGE attendees! Come to our Convention Operations room and get a parking sticker. Place this sticker on the back of your parking voucher. When you leave, you scan the front of the voucher then the back (sticker) to receive the discounted rate.
